## 7. Data Breach Response Plan

### Detection
- Automated monitoring (to be implemented)
- User reports
- Third-party notifications
- Security audits

### Assessment (Within 24 Hours)
1. Confirm breach occurred
2. Identify affected data
3. Assess number of data subjects impacted
4. Evaluate risk level
5. Determine cause and prevent further access

### Notification Requirements

**To Information Regulator** (If risk of harm exists):
- Within 72 hours of discovery
- Via prescribed form
- Include: nature of breach, categories of data, estimated numbers, consequences, measures taken

**To Data Subjects** (If high risk):
- As soon as reasonably possible
- Via email or direct communication
- Plain language explanation
- Steps taken to mitigate harm
- Recommendations for data subjects

**Documentation**:
- Maintain breach register
- Record all breaches (even minor)
- Document response actions
