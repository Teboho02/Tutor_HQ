## 7. Your Rights Under POPIA

### 7.1 Right to Access
You have the right to:
- Request confirmation of what personal information we hold
- Obtain a copy of your personal information
- Access your data in a structured, commonly used format

### 7.2 Right to Correction
You may:
- Update your profile information at any time
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- We will respond to correction requests within 30 days

### 7.3 Right to Deletion
You may request deletion of your personal information when:
- Data is no longer necessary for the purpose collected
- You withdraw consent and no other legal basis exists
- Data has been unlawfully processed
- Legal obligation requires deletion

**Exceptions:**
We may retain data when:
- Required by law (e.g., tax records)
- Necessary for legal claims
- Legitimate business purposes exist

### 7.4 Right to Object
You may object to:
- Processing for direct marketing purposes
- Processing based on legitimate interests
- Automated decision-making

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability
You may request:
- Your data in a machine-readable format
- Transfer of data to another service provider

### 7.6 Right to Restrict Processing
You may request restriction when:
- Contesting accuracy of data
- Processing is unlawful but you don't want deletion
- We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
- You may withdraw consent at any time
- Withdrawal does not affect prior processing
- May impact your ability to use Platform services

## 9. Data Retention

### 9.1 Retention Periods
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Retained for 2 years after last activity
- **Academic Records**: Retained for 5 years for reporting purposes
- **Financial Records**: Retained for 5 years per tax law requirements

### 9.2 Deletion Process
Upon account deletion or retention period expiry:
- Personal identifiers are permanently deleted
- Academic records may be anonymized for statistical purposes
- Backup copies are deleted within 90 days
